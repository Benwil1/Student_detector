This document outlines the standard operating procedures for handling employee performance reviews within the organization. The primary objective of this policy is to ensure that all reviews are conducted in a fair and transparent manner. To achieve this, supervisors must utilize the approved evaluation forms and adhere to the established timelines. It is crucial to provide employees with constructive feedback that highlights both their strengths and areas for improvement. Furthermore, all documentation related to the review process must be maintained in the employee's personnel file to ensure long-term accountability.
